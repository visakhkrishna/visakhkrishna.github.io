Digitalisation of Rolling Stock Inspection – The IQT project

An easy-to-use digital tool for inspection services in the railway industry

Nov 30th 2023 4 Min read

In this blogpost, I briefly describe the IQT project that I am currently developing along with my colleague at SYSTRA, Sweden. I simplify it in the following sections

What is IQT?

IQT is our mobile & web-based digital application that can be used to record inspections and send a report to the asset owner for their business requirements.

This is a part of our ongoing drive towards digitalisation of asset maintenance activities in the railway sector in Sweden. It has been developed after extensive survey of various stakeholders in the Swedish rail industry such as inspectors, asset-owners and maintainers.

How does it work?

The workflow is simple and easy to use. It consists of three components as described in the figure

Preparation Web application Mobile application
Rolling stock experts with domain knowledge prepare the layout of the asset or load it from an existing template as a simple list in excel or text file The web application processes the list and allocates components to specific areas of the asset and generates a work order with criteria to be fulfilled during inspection The workorder appears on an easy-to-use, intuitive interface on a mobile device for the inspectors on-site to enter comments, take photos, videos, and issue actions to be taken. This is simultaneously saved on an internet database linked to the web application.

Why are we making it and who is it for?

Plan Do Check Act.. *Better*

For industries with large and safety-critical assets such as the railway industry, quality management services are a vital part of the life-cycle activities. We follow the ISO55000-recommended PDCA cycle for continuous improvement in our asset management activities to realize maximum value from our assets.

For a complex asset as a railway vehicle with 100s of components to check, going around with a notepad isn't going to cut it exactly.

For a complex asset as a railway vehicle with 100s of components to check, going around with a notepad isn't going to cut it exactly. There is a need for an inspection tool that is mindful about the asset complexity, make it easier to register inspections, and also report it to the asset owner in a simple manner. With this vision in mind, the original application was developed for the PC by our team of ISO9000-certified rolling stock specialists and has been in use for more than a decade in Sweden. With the latest iteration we are just making it better and data-ready.

Your inspection data is more important than you think

What do we mean by data-ready? It simply means the preparedness and quality of data that can aid clear decision-making. It includes amongst other things such as data accuracy, completeness, timeliness, consistency, and accessibility. A well-labelled data collection system for an asset is crucial if you want the inspection to mean something beyond its completion.

Accumulating several inspections of the asset can help us create a digital picture of its life cycle and with every iteration get better at anticipating its maintenance requirements. In other words, it can be used to find patterns and tune maintenance activities. Well-labelled and accessible data forms the fundamental block towards building a digital twin. IQT is specifically tailored towards this requirement.

Such a tool can be useful for anyone in the industry who own, operate or maintain railway assets such as fleet-owners, rolling stock manufacturers, maintainers, infrastructure owners, railway consultancies, etc.

Our design principles:

SIMPLE FLEXIBLE FOCUSSED
The application is simple to use and intuitive. The setting up of the inspection worklist can be done with a simple text file with the component list The application works well for assets with varying complexities. Specially designed for rolling stock inspection activities in terms of number of components and reporting structure.
The web application is easily accessible from any electronic device and a basic inspection can be easily created with the available templates Easy to record, edit, delete, modify remarks for the user – making it easy to work collaboratively by different users and over a long time on an asset Focus on simple, efficient and annotated data collection which is important for data-driven diagnostics.

What do we offer?

  • Proprietary application tailor-made for rolling stock by our in-house experts
  • A iOS application designed for iPad mini along with the device
  • A web-based application for creating and reporting inspection reports
  • APIs to link our inspection data with analytics services
  • Support for application updates and general maintenance
